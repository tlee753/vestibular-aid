Caution

Any individual who uses these videos without the supervision of a treating therapist or a physician assumes the risk of injury resulting from performing the exercises. The creators and distributors disclaim any liability or loss in connection with the exercises and instruction.

Safety

  • Complete exercises in a comfortable chair
  • Perform each exercise no longer than 5 minutes with 3 minutes between each exercise
  • Stop immediately if becoming nauseated
  • If eye fatigue occurs, rest eyes loosely closed for 3 minutes
